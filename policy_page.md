# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-14

AnonPro ("we", "us", or "our") provides an anonymous social app. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## Data We Collect
- Account data: email, username/alias, display name, profile image.
- Content data: posts, comments, messages, and room activity.
- Device data: basic app/device info for troubleshooting.
- Notification data: FCM tokens for push notifications.

## How We Use Data
- Provide core features (posting, messaging, rooms, notifications).
- Prevent abuse and enforce community rules.
- Improve performance and reliability.

## Data Sharing
- We do not sell personal data.
- Data may be processed by third-party services (e.g., Supabase, Firebase) to operate the app.

## Your Choices
- You can update or delete your profile data in the app.
- You can manage notification preferences in Settings.
- You can contact support to request account deletion.

## Security
We use industry-standard security controls to protect data, but no system is 100% secure.

## Contact
For questions or requests, contact: support@anonpro.app
