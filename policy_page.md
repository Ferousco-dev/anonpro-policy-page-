# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

AnonPro ("we", "us", or "our") provides an anonymous social app. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## Data We Collect
- **Account data:** email, username/alias, display name, profile image.
- **Content data:** posts, comments, messages, and room activity.
- **Device data:** basic app/device info for troubleshooting.
- **Notification data:** FCM tokens for push notifications.

## How We Use Data
- Provide core features (posting, messaging, rooms, notifications).
- Prevent abuse and enforce community rules.
- Improve performance and reliability.
- **Content Moderation:** We actively moderate User-Generated Content (UGC). We may temporarily retain device identifiers or account data of banned users strictly to enforce bans and prevent them from re-registering.

## Data Sharing
- We do not sell personal data.
- Data may be processed by trusted third-party services (e.g., Supabase, Firebase) solely to operate the app functionalities.

## Your Choices
- You can update or delete your profile data directly in the app.
- You can manage your push notification preferences in Settings.
- You can contact support at any time to request complete account deletion.

## Security
We use industry-standard security controls and encrypted connections (HTTPS) to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

## Contact
For questions, concerns, or data deletion requests, contact us at: **support@anonpro.app**
